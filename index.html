69-Day Phase Plan
FoundationWeek 1–2
Learn the big lifts with strict form. Lighter weights while you dial in grip and control.
Progressive OverloadWeek 3–4
Add weight every session. Chest, arms and shoulders start showing real shape.
Max IntensityWeek 5–6
Push close to failure on key sets. Waist is smaller, upper body looks much denser.
⚡ Increase Weight When…
1
All reps, all sets are clean with your target grip → add 5 lbs next session.
2
Bench & Row: minimum 5–10 lbs added per week if technique holds.
3
Deadlift: +5–10 lbs every heavy session in weeks 1–4, then reassess.
4
Curls & Laterals: move up a dumbbell size once you hit top reps with full control.
5
Farmer's Carry: either walk further or go heavier every week. Full grip, no fingertips.
6
Log everything. "Sexy" is progressive overload plus consistency — not random effort.
